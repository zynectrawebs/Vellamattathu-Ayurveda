Our Founder

Vaidyan V N Sivaraman Nair

Vaidyan V. N. Sivaraman Nair, the visionary founder of Vellamattathu Ayurveda, began his journey in 1945 with a profound dedication to the principles of traditional Ayurvedic healing.

With deep-rooted knowledge and years of disciplined practice, he became a respected healer known for his compassionate care and commitment to holistic wellness.

Under his leadership, Vellamattathu Ayurveda grew into a trusted institution, attracting patients from near and far who sought authentic and effective Ayurvedic treatments.

His enduring legacy lives on through the continued work of the hospital, which upholds his values and remains a beacon of natural health and well-being.
