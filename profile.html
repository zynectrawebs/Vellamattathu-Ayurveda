About Us

Our Story

Vellamattathu Ayurveda was established in 1995 with a vision to provide authentic Ayurvedic treatments in a serene and healing environment. For over 25 years, we have been dedicated to preserving the traditional Ayurvedic practices while incorporating modern wellness approaches.

Our clinic is nestled in the heart of Kerala, known as the birthplace of Ayurveda. We take pride in our heritage and are committed to delivering holistic healing experiences that address the root causes of health issues rather than just treating symptoms.

At Vellamattathu Ayurveda, we believe in the power of nature to heal and restore balance. Our treatments are based on ancient Ayurvedic texts and are customized to meet the unique needs of each individual.

Our Founder

Dr. Arun Kumar

Dr. Arun Kumar is a renowned Ayurvedic physician with over 25 years of experience in traditional Ayurvedic practices. After completing his degree in Ayurvedic Medicine from the prestigious Ayurveda College in Kerala, he dedicated his life to bringing authentic Ayurvedic treatments to people seeking natural healing solutions.

His journey began when he witnessed the remarkable healing powers of Ayurveda in his own family. This personal experience inspired him to delve deeper into the ancient science and eventually establish Vellamattathu Ayurveda.

Dr. Kumar has trained numerous Ayurvedic practitioners who now serve in various parts of the world. He regularly conducts workshops and seminars to spread awareness about the benefits of Ayurveda and has authored several books on the subject.

His vision for Vellamattathu Ayurveda was born from a desire to create a sanctuary where ancient wisdom meets modern wellness needs. Under his guidance, our clinic has helped thousands of patients achieve balance and healing through personalized Ayurvedic care.
